MU CS Research activities 2022-10-01 to 2023-09-30

Summary

MU CS Department published 50+ publications in top journals and conferences venues during this 2022-23 reporting period. The department has several areas of excellence, notably for the period, in Theoretical Computer Science, Evolutionary Computation, Signal Processing, Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition, Computer Science Education and Remote Sensing.

During the period, 2M€+ was awarded to the department in research funding from several funding agencies (IRC, SFI, EPA). The MU CS department is also well represented as part of SFI research centres (LERO, ADAPT, IFORM, CLIMATE+).

The CS department is also very engaged in a variety of outreach activities supporting computer science education in Ireland. For instance, the CS MU department organizes beginner and advanced summer camps cater for students aged 13-18 years old that include Women-in-STEM week. The CS department PACT team has run continuing professional development workshops on computational thinking for primary and secondary school teachers.

🏆 The SFI funded project copilotai.ie has won the SFI-Defense Innovation Challenge prize (see press release Tánaiste and Minister reveal million-euro idea in SFI-Defence Organisation Innovation Challenge). This project aims at developing a tactical decision-support system, using the latest Artificial Intelligence technologies for managing Wildfires.

Publications

Note that conference publications are peer-reviewed and are considered as high quality publications, that are sometimes ranked higher than journal publications. We use Google scholar rankings in the category Engineering and Computer Science (unless otherwise specified) to comment below on the quality of the conferences and journal venues.

From the Maynooth University Computer Science Department academic staff's publications in the period 2022-10-01 to 2023-09-30 (listed below), several publications were accepted in high ranked conferences such as:

  • GECCO (Genetic and Evolutionary Computation Conference) is a high ranked peer-reviewed conference in the subcategory Evolutionary Computation.

  • The Annual Conference on Innovation and Technology in Computer Science Education is a A ranked conference according to conferenceranks.com.
