Publications

Methodology: We use Google scholar rankings in the category Engineering and Computer Science to comment below on the quality of the conferences and journal venues.

Note that conference publications are peer-reviewed and are considered as high quality publications, that are sometimes ranked higher than journal publications.

Listed below are the Maynooth University Computer Science Department academic staff's publications in the period 2022-10-01 to 2023-09-30.

In that period several publications were accepted in high ranked conferences such as:

  • Logical Methods in Computer Science is a high ranked peer-reviewed conference in the subcategory Theoretical Computer Science.
  • GECCO (Genetic and Evolutionary Computation Conference) is a high ranked peer-reviewed conference in the subcategory Evolutionary Computation.
  • INTERSPEECH is a high ranked peer-reviewed conference in the subcategory Signal Processing.
  • CVPR is the top conference in the subcategory Computer Vision and Pattern Recognition. This conference has several satellite workshops and staff member John McDonald was also amongst the organising committee of 4th Omnidirectional Computer Vision workshop in 2023.
  • STL (IEEE Spoken Language Technology Workshop) is a well ranked peer-reviewed conference in the subcategory Computational Linguistics.
  • The International Archives of the Photogrammetry, Remote Sensing and Spatial Information Sciences is a well ranked peer-reviewed conference in the subcategory remote sensing.
