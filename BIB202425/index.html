MU CS Research activities 2024-10-01 to 2025-09-30

Summary

MU CS Department published 50+ publications in top journals and conferences venues during this 2024-25 reporting period. The department has several areas of excellence, notably for the period, in Theoretical Computer Science, DNA computing, Evolutionary Computation, Computer Vision, AI and deep learning, perception, virtual reality, Spectroscopy, Computer Science Education, and Remote Sensing.

The CS department is also very engaged in a variety of outreach activities supporting computer science education in Ireland. For instance, the CS MU department organizes beginner and advanced summer camps cater for students aged 13-18 years old that include Women-in-STEM week. The CS department PACT team has run continuing professional development workshops on computational thinking for primary and secondary school teachers.

Examples of impactful research of MU CS researchers for society include:

  • Maynooth University's Terrain-AI Researchers Collaborate with Kildare County Council on Award-Winning `Grass-to-Gas` Project
  • contributions to the Oireachtas Joint Committee for Transportation Drones

Maynooth University CS Department is also contributing to the organization of events:

  • Workshop on Advancing Enterprise Modelling through Digital Transformation, FAIR Data Management, and Blockchain Integration (PoEM 2025 - Practice of Enterprise Modeling, Geneva, Switzerland).
  • OSM Science 2025 track of the global OpenStreetMap State of the Map conference which takes place in Manilla, the Philippines Oct 3rd - Oct 5th. The State of the Map conference attracts around 500 delegates annually.
